# Title - Short phrase that describes the key decision.

**Date:** When the decision was made.

**Status:** Current status of the decision (i.e. proposed, accepted, deprecated, superseded or rejected).

## Summary

**In the context of** functional requirement or architecture characteristic,
**facing** non-functional requirement or quality attribute,
**we decided** selected option,
**neglecting** alternative options,
**to achieve** benefits,
**accepting** drawbacks.

## Context

Describe the facts that influence the decision. State these in value-neutral language.

## Decision

Explain how the decision will solve the problem, in light of the facts presented in the context.

## Consequences

Describe the impact of the decision. What becomes easier and harder to do? There will be positive,
neutral and negative consequences, identify all of them.
